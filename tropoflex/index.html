Injections and Observations

This page is the gateway for collecting TropoFlex™ injection and observation data.

It is intended for the use of veterinarians, technicians, owners, trainers, advocates, and other stakeholders to collect data relevant to the administration and efficacy of TropoFlex™ treatment regimens, as well as pertinent narrative and multimedia demonstrating observed performance, behavior, and temperament changes.

If you have any questions, comments, suggestions, or feedback regarding these data collection tools, please contact Jim Gasser at jim@tropoflexinc.com.
